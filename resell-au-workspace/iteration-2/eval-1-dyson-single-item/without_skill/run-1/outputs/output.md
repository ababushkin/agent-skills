## Seller notes

**Photos that close listings faster**
- Photo 1: Full unit standing upright — clean background, good natural light.
- Photo 2: All attachments laid out flat together (shows buyers everything is present).
- Photo 3: Close-up of the motor head bristles (buyers look for wear here).
- Photo 4: Charger and docking bracket visible.
- Bonus: A short 10-second video on Facebook Marketplace showing it powering on and picking something up eliminates 80% of "does it still work?" messages.

**Reduce time-wasters**
- State "local pickup only" clearly — filters interstate lowballers.
- "Happy to demonstrate" in the description means buyers who show up are serious.
- Use platform messaging first; don't give out your phone number in the listing.

**Timing**
- List Thursday evening or Friday morning — weekend browsing drives most sales.
- If no enquiries by Day 7, relist at $199 and note "price reduced."

**Negotiation**
- If someone opens at $150, counter at $210 — the gap is too large to split cleanly in one move.
- A buyer offering $180–200 on a $220 ask is a realistic sale; meet at $200 and close it.

**Platform**
- List on Facebook Marketplace and Gumtree simultaneously (free on both).
- Share the FB Marketplace listing into 2–3 Melbourne buy/swap/sell groups for faster reach.
- FB Marketplace typically moves Dyson vacuums faster than Gumtree in Melbourne due to higher traffic.
