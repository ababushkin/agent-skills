## FIRE-SALE ITEMS

---

### Box of ~50 mixed DVDs — DO NOT LIST SOLO

**Routing: Garage-sale bundle or curbside free box**

The AU secondhand DVD market has effectively collapsed. Individual discs fetch $0.50–$2 each at op-shops if they're popular titles; bulk lots of 50 mixed/unknown titles on Gumtree routinely list at $10–$20 for the whole box and still don't sell quickly. Net return for the effort of listing, meeting buyers, and negotiating is not worth it.

**Next best actions (pick one):**
1. **Garage sale box** — price the whole box at $10–$15 cash, let people rummage. Anything left goes to the footpath.
2. **Free curbside box** — put a "FREE — take all or some" sign on it and they'll be gone in an hour.
3. **Donate** — Salvos/Vinnies/Brotherhood of St Laurence accept DVDs. Worth it if you want them gone cleanly without a garage sale.

If any titles are obviously collectible (rare box sets, Criterion, out-of-print cult films), pull those out before the free box and check individually — a single rare disc can fetch $20–$50.

---

### CRT Monitor 19" (works) — DO NOT LIST

**Routing: Hard rubbish / e-waste / free pickup**

CRT monitors are firmly in the "effort > reward" category. They're heavy (typically 15–20 kg for a 19"), awkward to transport, and have near-zero buyer demand — the entire category has been replaced. Even working units rarely sell on AU Marketplace; listings that do move go for $0–$10 and often require the seller to help carry it to the buyer's car.

**Next best actions (pick one):**
1. **Hard rubbish collection** — put it out on your council's next scheduled collection.
2. **E-waste drop-off** — most councils and many Officeworks / Jaycar locations accept CRT monitors for free e-waste recycling. Check [recyclingnearyou.com.au](https://www.recyclingnearyou.com.au) for your nearest drop-off.
3. **Free pickup listing** — if you'd rather not move it yourself, list it as "FREE — you collect, you carry" on FB Marketplace. Retro gaming/computing hobbyists occasionally grab working CRTs for emulation setups. Price: $0. Zero effort beyond the post.

Do not attempt to sell it for cash — it will sit, take up space, and eventually go the same route.
