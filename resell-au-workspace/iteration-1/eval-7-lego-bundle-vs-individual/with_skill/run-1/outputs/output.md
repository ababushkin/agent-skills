## Bundle vs Individual Recommendation

**Sell separately. Do not bundle these three.**

These are three distinct, high-value retired sets with their own collector audiences — a bundle would either sit unsold (wrong buyer pool) or force you to accept a blended price that undersells the Chiron and the Tower Bridge. Each set has meaningful standalone value and will attract a different buyer. The Chiron in particular is a sought-after Technic collector piece even incomplete; bundling it with City sets muddies the listing and buries the search signal. List all three separately, run them concurrently on Facebook Marketplace and Gumtree, and manage each offer on its own merits.

The only bundling scenario worth considering: if the Police Station sits for 3+ weeks with no bites, you could pair it with a small City add-on or drop to garage-sale price — but not with these other two.

## Cross-item notes

1. **Chiron missing pieces: must disclose, must price for it.** Do not list it as "complete" or "excellent condition" with a footnote. Put the missing pieces in the title itself. Technic buyers check every bag — if they discover missing parts on pickup you'll lose the sale or face a dispute.

2. **Tower Bridge no box: real money lost, not just cosmetic.** New-sealed examples sell at 2× your likely asking price. The box is half the value for collector-grade retired sets. Disclose and price accordingly — don't try to match sealed-box pricing.

3. **Timing:** No strong seasonal angle on any of these. LEGO collector sets sell year-round. All three are good to list immediately. Run them concurrently — different buyer pools, no cannibalism.

4. **Photography tip for all three:** Natural light on a clean surface. For the Chiron and Tower Bridge, consider a "parts laid out" photo alongside any built/partially-built shots — shows completeness (or honest incompleteness) at a glance.

5. **Payment/no-hold reminder:** Cash or PayID on pickup. Don't hold any of these for anyone without a deposit — the Chiron and Tower Bridge will attract time-wasters at these price points.
