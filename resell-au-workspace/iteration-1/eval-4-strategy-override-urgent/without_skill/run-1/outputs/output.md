## Recommended Pricing

### Strategy: "Urgent but not desperate"
With a Friday deadline, you want to attract buyers immediately while not signalling panic (which invites lowball offers). Price 10–15% below the mid-market range, state the deadline clearly, and firm up if you get multiple enquiries.

| Item | Ask Price | Absolute Floor | Rationale |
|------|-----------|---------------|-----------|
| KitchenAid 5Qt Red Mixer | **$260** | $200 | Below mid-market, still credible for a working unit |
| LG 65" OLED C9 + original box | **$850** | $650 | Original box justifies premium; urgency gets it moving |

**Bundling option:** If the same buyer wants both, offer $1,050 (saves them $60, clears both in one transaction — ideal given your timeline).

## Posting Tactics for a Fast Sale

### Timing
- **Post today or tomorrow morning** — do not wait. Weekend posts get the most traffic on both platforms.
- Refresh / repost listings on Wednesday if no sale yet (Gumtree allows relisting; FB allows editing the post which bumps it algorithmically).

### Platform split
| Platform | Priority | Why |
|----------|----------|-----|
| Facebook Marketplace | Primary | Higher volume, faster local reach, chat is frictionless |
| Gumtree | Secondary | Still strong for appliances and electronics in AU |
| Buy/Swap/Sell Facebook Groups | Bonus | Search your suburb + "buy swap sell" — free, fast |

### Handling offers
- Expect lowballs at 60–70% of your ask. Counter at 90%.
- For the TV especially: if someone offers $700+, accept it. Missing a $150 difference is cheaper than paying for storage or disposal.
- State "first to inspect and pay takes it" — creates urgency on the buyer's side.

### Safety
- Meet in your driveway or building lobby (not inside your home if possible while packing).
- Cash or instant bank transfer (PayID/Osko) — no "I'll pay when I pick up in 3 days."
- Confirm pickup time by message before committing to hold the item.

### If Friday arrives with no sale
- **KitchenAid:** Drop to $180 and repost. A working mixer at that price will clear within hours.
- **LG TV:** Drop to $700 and repost. Contact any previous enquirers directly.
- **Last resort:** Marketplace platforms like AirTasker or Facebook "free to good home" groups if you need it physically gone before the truck arrives.
